About Our Company

Postertext started out as a gift idea for a well-read friend. An image of the gift was posted online where it quickly spread by word-of-mouth. Within a week, we received nearly a hundred requests to buy the art print or to have their favorite books done in our book poster style. Postertext opened its store a few weeks later on July 7, 2010 and have been in business ever since.

The company is composed of a team of talented artists and avid readers from all over the world. We share the common goal of capturing the greatest works of literature in illustrative form. With our art prints, you can decorate your walls with something beautiful and literary. 

We hope you enjoy our art prints as much as we enjoy designing them!
